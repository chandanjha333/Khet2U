Frequently Asked Questions

What are your delivery hours?
We deliver Monday through Saturday from 8am to 8pm. For specific delivery time slots, you can select your preferred time during checkout.
How can I track my order?
Once your order is confirmed, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can use this number on our website or mobile app to track your delivery in real-time.
What is your return policy?
If you're not satisfied with your purchase, you can return it within 7 days. For fresh products, please notify us within 24 hours of delivery if there are any quality issues.
Do you offer same-day delivery?
Yes, we offer same-day delivery for orders placed before 2pm, subject to availability and your delivery location.
How do I change or cancel my order?
You can modify or cancel your order up to 2 hours before the scheduled delivery time through your account dashboard or by contacting our customer service.
